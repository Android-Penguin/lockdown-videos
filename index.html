Introduction

Over the 4 weeks of level 4 lockdown, PB tech hosted a series of video competitions. Each day had a Taskmaster style challenge where people had to create a 1 minute video to achieve the day's brief in the most creative way possible. The challenges ranged from building elabourate contraptions to doing the most of something in a 7 second time limit, with the best video from each day winning a tech product valued between $100-$1000.

I entered 6 of these challenges and was able to win 2 of them. Each video had at least a day's work put into the planning, filming and editing; and I have created this website to show all the finished products, as well as some behind the scenes footage.
