Southern Alps in 6.68 seconds

Task: Stack as many things as possible on the back of your hand in 7 seconds.

After a bit of experimenting, I found the best objects were playing cards. The least slipery cards we had were decorated with mountains on the back, which inspired the skit we used in the video.

The winner was decided by the person who could stack the most items in the time limit and we came runner up with a total of 22 cards (Winning video beat us by 1 staple).
